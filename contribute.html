Contribute to Heritage Harbour

Your contributions can make a significant difference in preserving our shared cultural heritage. There are several ways you can contribute to Heritage Harbour:

  • Donate: We accept donations of cultural artefacts, provided they are legally obtained and owned. Each donation helps expand our collection and preserve more of our shared heritage.
  • Volunteer: We are always in need of volunteers to help catalogue new items, conduct research, and assist with our preservation efforts.
  • Spread the Word: Help raise awareness about cultural preservation by sharing our mission with your friends, family, and social networks.

To learn more about how you can contribute, please contact us. We appreciate your support in our mission to preserve cultural heritage.
